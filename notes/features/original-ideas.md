## Round 1 — Niche Recovery Features

### The Pause
90-second interrupt screen. Triggered manually or when craving scores spike. Single large button, countdown timer. Can't skip. Forces the biological window where urges peak and fade. Based on urge surfing science. Offline, no dependencies.

### Pattern Alerts
AI silently tracks craving spikes by time of day, day of week, emotional state. After 2–3 weeks: "Your hardest window is Friday 6–10pm, 4 weeks running. Want to schedule something then?" YOUR specific fingerprint, not generic advice.

### Message From Past Self (Time Capsule)
On day 1 (or any milestone), record a voice message or write a letter to yourself. Sealed and locked until a future milestone you choose. When it hits, the app unlocks it. One of the most emotionally resonant things a recovery app could do.

### Tiny Wins Log
Separate from journaling. 5-second micro-entries: "Walked past the bar." "Called sponsor instead." Timestamped, encrypted, private. Builds a "wins streak" independently of check-ins.

### Emergency Bridge
One tap sends a pre-written "I need support right now" SMS to a designated contact. No typing. Works for SMS offline. Cuts friction that costs lives at 2am. *(Note: now partially incorporated into AI sponsor's real human bridge feature)*

### Night Check
Targeted late-night micro-check-in (10pm–midnight). 3 taps: how are you right now + one immediate coping suggestion. Addresses highest-risk window without full check-in UI.

## Round 2 — Today's Standard (2026 app expectations)

### Home Screen Widget
Sobriety counter + daily quote + streak. iOS + Android. #1 thing users expect from any habit/wellness app.

### Lock Screen Widget
Clean time on lock screen. One number. Constant reminder, zero friction.

### App Icon Quick Actions
Long-press app icon → shortcuts: "Log Check-in", "Call Sponsor", "I Need Help". Standard iOS/Android, recovery apps never implement it properly.

### Live Activity / Dynamic Island
Milestone approaching or check-in due → surfaced in Dynamic Island / notification bar. iOS 16+ standard.

### Health App Integration
Apple Health / Google Fit. Pull sleep + steps data. Correlate with mood check-ins. "You averaged 4hrs sleep the nights before your high-craving days." Expected in 2026.

### Swipeable Daily Card Stack
Morning content as swipeable stack — reading, affirmation, today's challenge. Like a daily story format. Every content app does this now.

### Siri / Google Assistant Shortcuts
"Hey Siri, how many days am I sober?" "Hey Google, log my morning check-in." Expected from any utility app in 2026.
